About Us

The Iza Travels Story

Founded in 2013, Iza Travels has been providing exceptional taxi services in Kottayam with a strong focus on customer satisfaction, reliability, and safety.

What started as a small local taxi service in Kottayam has grown into a trusted transportation partner for individuals, families, businesses, and tourists throughout Kerala. We specialize in Kottayam taxi services, Cochin Airport transfers, and comfortable rides to popular tourism destinations like Munnar, Kumarakom, Idukki, and Kovalam.

Our mission is simple: to provide safe, comfortable, and punctual cab services at competitive prices while maintaining the highest levels of professionalism. Whether you need a reliable taxi for local trips in Kottayam, airport transfers to Cochin International Airport, or interstate taxi services to Bangalore and Chennai, we’ve got you covered.

We take pride in our well-maintained fleet of vehicles and our team of professional drivers who are committed to making every journey a pleasant and hassle-free experience. Choose Iza Travels for the best taxi service in Kottayam and Kerala.
